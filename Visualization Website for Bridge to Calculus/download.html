We would like to share the data with you!

It is impossible to solve the problem of educational inequality only by through groups such as Bridge to Calculus. But it is possible for people to educate themselves on this problem by studying the data and seeing what needs attention. While doing this project, we were not only thinking about the assignment when reviewing the data, but also the implications of it. We realized the importance of groups like Btc. So, we want to share all of our data resources that we used, in hopes it may spread awareness and knowledge to others.
